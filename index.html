The Seven Kandas

I Bala Kanda The divine birth, youth, and sacred marriage of Rama. II Ayodhya Kanda The coronation undone, the exile accepted, and dharma tested. III Aranya Kanda The forest years, the golden deer, and Sita's abduction. IV Kishkindha Kanda The Vanara alliance and Hanuman's awakening strength. V Sundara Kanda Hanuman's ocean leap, Sita's discovery, and Lanka ablaze. VI Yuddha Kanda The bridge, the war, and the fall of Ravana. VII Uttara Kanda Rama Rajya, Sita's return to Earth, and the final ascent.
