The Book of Youth · 77 Sargas · Chapters of Origin

In Ayodhya on the banks of the Sarayu, King Dasharatha prays for an heir. Heaven answers — and an eternal story begins.

Valmiki, foremost among sages, deep in austerity and Vedic learning, approached the divine sage Narada — best among those who wield words — and asked his eternal question.

VII

Uttara Kanda

The Final Book · 111 Sargas · Rama's Reign and Return

Rama's rule becomes the ideal of all governance. But dharma's burden is never lifted from the righteous. The final chapter carries the deepest sorrow — and the most transcendent ending.

Trees forever in flower, forever bearing fruit, always shaded and cool. Winds blow gentle and fragrant with the sweetness of flowers.
